# Superstore Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

A performance dashboard for a US retail superstore, tracking sales, profit, and returns across product categories and states, with year-over-year comparison.

**File:** `superstore-analytics-dashboard.pbix`

## Headline numbers

| Metric | Current | vs. prior year |
|---|---|---|
| Sales | $1.17M | +40.07% |
| Profit | $136.37K | +51.35% |
| Return rate | 11.26% | -5.77% |

## What stands out

Technology carries the business. Copiers, phones, and accessories deliver the strongest profit. Furniture is the drag, and Tables in particular run negative margins even while they sell. Returns improved year over year, and the sales trend climbs steadily from 2020 through 2023.

## Stack

Power BI Desktop: data model, DAX measures, interactive visuals.
